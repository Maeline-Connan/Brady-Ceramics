Michel Brady

An Irish ceramist based in Brittany, I make utilitarian pottery on the wheel.

St. Amand's stoneware pieces are created with the idea of offering you the pleasure of drinking or eating in handmade pottery.

In ceramics, I appreciate, and try to emphasize in my work, a sober, elegant aesthetic that appeals to the touch.

I learned to make glazes from Annie Metzger (Terre de Lune), whose Japanese style has influenced my own work.
